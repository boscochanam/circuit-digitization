The pipeline repository is available at \url{https://github.com/boscochanam/circuit-digitization}. The trained 16-class component detector (YOLO26M-OBB) is released at \url{https://huggingface.co/boscochanam/circuit-component-detector} and was trained on the public CGHD-1152 corpus \cite{Thoma2021}. The evaluation set of 134 manually verified hand-drawn images, with paired component and wire annotations (3{,}524 wire segments), is maintained by the authors. The synthetic ground-truth circuit catalog and the evaluation harness are part of the repository. Benchmark outputs, figure assets, and manuscript source files are contained in the project workspace.
